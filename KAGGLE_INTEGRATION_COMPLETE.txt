🆘 QUICK TROUBLESHOOTING
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Issue: "Kaggle CLI not found"
Solution: pip install kaggle

Issue: "API key not found"
Solution: 
   1. Download from https://www.kaggle.com/settings/account
   2. Save to ~/.kaggle/kaggle.json
   3. chmod 600 ~/.kaggle/kaggle.json

Issue: "Not authenticated"
Solution: Verify .kaggle/kaggle.json exists and has correct permissions

Issue: "No audio files found"
Solution:
   1. Check download completed: ls respiratory_data_raw/
   2. Check files exist: find respiratory_data_raw -name "*.wav"
   3. Manually extract: unzip respiratory_data_raw/*.zip

Issue: Slow download
Solution: Normal speed 1-3 MB/sec, expected time 15-40 minutes

See KAGGLE_SETUP.md for detailed troubleshooting
