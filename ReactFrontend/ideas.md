## Selected Direction

**Chosen philosophy:** Bioluminescent Clinical Interface.

This direction best matches the user's request for a site that feels clinically credible, technically impressive, and visually dramatic without relying on generic startup tropes. It supports a dark diagnostic atmosphere, gives the Three.js hero a natural visual logic, and provides a coherent motion language for the pipeline, audio visualizer, and research sections.

## Implementation Commitments

- The interface will use an asymmetric, chamber-like scroll structure instead of a generic centered landing page.
- Typography will pair **Space Grotesk** with **Manrope** and **JetBrains Mono**, explicitly avoiding Inter-heavy defaults.
- Surfaces will use cold glass, instrument-panel borders, signal traces, and controlled cyan/violet illumination.
- Motion will resemble breathing, scanning, telemetry, and inference rather than decorative marketing animation.
- When making design choices, the guiding question will be: **Does this reinforce diagnostic precision and computational trust, or does it dilute it?**

## Asset Note

Because the brief forbids photography and asks for custom interface-driven visuals, any generated assets used later should be abstract, non-photographic, and integrated as atmospheric sci-fi medical textures rather than human or stock-style imagery.
